What is Canvas Carnival?

Canvas Carnival is a small businnes located right in the heart of Galway! We are hosting different kinds of painting events every week.

If you are looking for fun , unique, enjoyable and creative experience, look no further! Join us at Canvas Carnival for an unforgettable time. We believe that everyone has an inner artist within themselves! Unleash your artistic spirit in a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere, surrounded by fellow art enthusiasts. Our state-of-the-art studio is equipped with high-quality materials and supplies, ensuring that you have everything you need to bring your vision to life.

Let the colors flow and let your creativity shine!
